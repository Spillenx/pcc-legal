Data Deletion

Last updated: January 8, 2026

Pocket Currency Converter stores data locally on your device using SwiftData and may sync it to your private iCloud (CloudKit) storage if enabled.

You can delete your data by:

  • Using any in-app delete/clear options (if provided),
  • Disabling iCloud sync for the App in device Settings (if you no longer want sync), and
  • Uninstalling the App to remove locally stored data from your device.
